About Msika-Link

Msika-Link is a community-first platform made for local market vendors who want visibility online. Our mission is to bridge traditional market trade with modern digital convenience.

Many local businesses sell high-quality products but struggle to be seen outside walk-in customers. Msika-Link solves this by giving each vendor a digital storefront, product highlights, and contact visibility.

Who We Serve

We serve shoppers looking for quality local products and small business owners who want affordable digital exposure.

Our Impact Goal

We aim to grow community trade by helping sellers attract repeat customers, improve trust, and increase daily sales through consistent online visibility.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I list my stall on Msika-Link?

    Contact us through phone, WhatsApp, or email with your business name, category, and sample product photos.

    Does Msika-Link handle online payments?

    Currently, buyers and sellers arrange payment and delivery terms directly to keep the process flexible and local.

    Can I advertise seasonal products?

    Yes. Vendors can update listings for seasonal stock, promotions, and limited-time offers to attract more customers.

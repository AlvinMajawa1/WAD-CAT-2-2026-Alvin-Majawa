Conversation Summary – Msika-Link Website

I had a short conversation with Kelvin Kamuzu Banda, co-founder of the Msika-Link platform, 
one of the early online marketplace initiatives focused on connecting local traders and customers.

He explained that Msika-Link was created to help small and medium market vendors get digital visibility, 
attract more buyers, and promote local business growth. He emphasized that many traders have quality products 
but struggle to market themselves online in a simple and affordable way.

From our discussion, the purpose of this webpage is to present Msika-Link as a trusted local marketplace brand, 
showcase key product categories, feature real vendors, and make it easy for potential customers and sellers to 
reach out quickly.

Target Audience:
- Local customers looking for trusted products from nearby markets.
- Small business owners and market vendors who want to list their products online.
- Community members interested in supporting local trade.

Design and Content Preferences from Kelvin:
- A clean, modern, and professional design.
- Strong use of marketplace images to make the page visually engaging.
- Mobile-friendly layout since many users browse on phones.
- Clear section structure (About, Categories, Gallery, Vendors, Contact).
- Easy contact options (Call/WhatsApp and Email).
- Warm but business-focused colors that reflect trust, growth, and community.

Expected Visitor Action:
- Buyers should explore products and contact vendors.
- Vendors should be encouraged to join Msika-Link and request onboarding/listing support.
